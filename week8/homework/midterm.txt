Trademark & Color: Especially the Color Grey

Throughout my quest for a peculiarly unique “grey zone” in Media & Intellectual Property Law, I finally found that what I was looking for was under my nose the whole time. It turns out that “grey,” although not technically a color, eventually led me to the strange world of trademarked colors.
